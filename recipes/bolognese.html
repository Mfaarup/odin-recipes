Bolognese

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef - chunks or minced
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 large onion or 2 small
  • A heap tablespoon of tomato pure
  • 1 or 2 sticks of celery
  • 1 large carrot
  • 300-400 ml of beef stock
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 or 2 raw salciccia sausages
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika
  • 150 gram of pancetta

Steps

  1. Finely dice the onion
  2. Finely dice the celery
  3. Grate the carrot
  4. Mince or chop the garlic
  5. If beef chunks - chop into bite size pieces
  6. Add a tablespoon of oil to a large pot or sautepan and turn the heat to medium
  7. When the pan is hot add the onion and stir the onion to make sure they don't burn
  8. Add the celery and carrots a minute after the onion
  9. Stir occasionaly - let it cook gently for 5 minutes
  10. Add salt and pepper - stir afterwards
  11. Add the garlic and stir while cooking it - otherwise it will burn
  12. Add the beef and stir occasionaly - It is advised to let it get some browning, as it will add to the flavour
  13. Add the tomato pure and stir it into the mix
  14. After about a minute and the chopped tomatoes
  15. After another few minutes add the beef stock
  16. Turn down the heat to a gentle simmer and put on the lid
  17. Let it sit for up to 3 hours - The longer the better, but will be fine after 15 minutes
  18. Cook your pasta or spaghetti according to instructions just before serving
